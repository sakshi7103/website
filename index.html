About Us

Welcome to iCREAM, where we believe that life is better with a scoop of ice cream in hand! Located in the heart of New York, our family-owned ice cream shop is dedicated to bringing you the freshest, creamiest, and most delicious ice cream in town.

Our mission is simple: to create sweet moments that bring people together. Whether it's a first date, a family outing, or a solo treat after a long day, we strive to make every visit to our shop a special one. We’re passionate about ice cream, and we can't wait to share that joy with you!
